Call Prep

3 Things to Bring to Your Call

Exactly what to prepare, who should attend, and how to make the most of your 30-minute consultation.

This call is for you if:

  • You are an accredited investor (net worth $1M+ or income $200K+)
  • You have $100,000 or more to deploy in the next 12 months
  • You are looking for monthly passive income, tax advantages, or portfolio diversification
  • You want to invest directly with the operator, not through a platform or middleman
  • You are comfortable with a multi-year hold period and illiquid investment

This call is not for you if:

  • You are not an accredited investor
  • You are looking to invest less than $100,000
  • You need immediate liquidity or a short-term investment
  • You are looking for guaranteed returns (no real estate investment is risk-free)
  • You are not prepared to make a decision within a reasonable timeframe

If any of the above apply, please reschedule. We would rather save both of our time. Reschedule here.
