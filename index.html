How Your Investment Works

Four pillars. One vertically integrated platform. Complete investor alignment.

1

We Source and Underwrite

We identify high-growth Texas multifamily communities using deep broker relationships and our proprietary underwriting model. Every deal must pass a minimum 1.50x Loaded Interest Coverage Ratio before we will acquire.

2

We Acquire With Conservative Structure

Fixed-rate, interest-only debt at a maximum 65% loan-to-value. Over $600M in financing from Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac, and select private lenders.

3

We Operate In-House

Our 200+ person team at Radiance Living manages every property. Leasing, maintenance, construction, accounting. No outsourced management. Full control, full accountability.

4

You Earn

Monthly cash flow distributions backed by an 8% compounding preferred return. Tax deductions through depreciation and cost segregation. Capital appreciation. And when we sell or refinance, you share in the upside.
